Welcome to Humantra

Humantra is a modern rock and grunge band from Zagreb, Croatia. Experience our raw sound, powerful vocals, and gritty aesthetic. Listen to our latest singles Aha and Borderline.

About the Band

Formed in 2021, Humantra is a three-piece band consisting of Morana Bing (vocals), Mauro Cherin (drums), and Vedran Semenski (guitar). Our music blends classic grunge with modern alternative rock energy.
